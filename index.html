Corsi Block
Tapping Test

A standardised measure of visuospatial working memory. Takes about 2–3 minutes.

How it works: A sequence of blocks will light up one by one.

Your job: Tap them in the exact same order after the sequence finishes.

Rounds: Each round gets longer. You have 2 lives. Lose both and the test ends.

Important: Do this alone, in a quiet place. No distractions.
